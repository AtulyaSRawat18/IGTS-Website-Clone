Secure Your Seat at the Table.

Membership in the Indian Game Theory Society is reserved for those who see the board before the pieces move. We are recruiting for the 2024-25 cycle.

Division of Strategy

The analytical core of the society. Candidates must demonstrate proficiency in Nash Equilibria, zero-sum dynamics, and competitive modeling. We seek the architects of logic who can deconstruct complex geopolitical and economic systems into solvable frameworks.

♥

The Diplomatic Corps

Game theory is not just math; it is the study of human interaction. The Corps manages the society's external relations, negotiation simulations, and high-level collaborative research. We value persuasion, empathy-mapping, and the art of the 'Pareto Optimal' compromise.

♦

Economic Modeling

Understanding markets as biological organisms. This division focuses on incentive structures, auction theory, and stochastic processes. Candidates should be comfortable with calculus and the ruthless efficiency of resource allocation in scarce environments.
